Health Tech

After growing at a CAGR of 39% from FY20 to FY23, the healthtech market in India is estimated to reach $50Bn by 2033. Indians are also projected to spend an estimated USD 363 billion on HealthTech by 2025.

Investment
Thesis

Healthtech is arguably one of those sectors that has seen user resistance for the longest time. However, since the pandemic, people have started realising the importance of preventive HealthTech, speciality care, health insurance, and diagnostics.

Moreover, state initiatives such as the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) are giving a push to the industry as well as further removing public inhibitions. One aspect still remains to be seen though, whether it will be feasible and accessible to the common public or just for a meagre few and there is definitely a lot to look forward to.
